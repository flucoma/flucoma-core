Datasets:
- blobs : Three blobs distributed randomly
- circles: Two centric circles
- flucoma_corpus_*: pre-analysed MFCCs of the FluCoMa demo audio files in small slices
- help.reg.*: pre-trained mlp regressor for the helpfile
- gaussian4: Four evenly distributed gaussian masses
- line: A single thinly distributed line
- mammoth: a 3d dataset in a shape of a mammoth (source: https://pair-code.github.io/understanding-umap/)
- moon : Two overlapping crescent shapes
